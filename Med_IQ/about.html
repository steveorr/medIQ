Why MedIQ?


    The idea of MedIQ was started by three Northwestern University students as a capstone project
    in Spring of 2018. Abosede, Emmanuel, and Steve were concerned about the many botched
    medical procedures that occur annually in the country’s medical facilities. Their solution is
    MedIQ, an application that give patients a platform to identify the most qualified medical
    practitioners to handle their procedures. In the process, experienced doctors can also be sure
    that their services are getting noticed. That is the kind of relieve the three students sorted
    to deliver to the health industry.
